Did you receive permission to ADD an online/hybrid course?

Open the instructor's email, click "forward" and send it to "online@wlac.edu", if the instructor didn't copy us on their reply to you.
Include the course name and section number in the subject line.
Place your full name, student ID number, phone number, and the email address on file within the Student Information System (SIS), somewhere within the body of the email.
