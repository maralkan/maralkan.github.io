Seeking God

Psalm 119:1-2

Blessed means "superlatively" happy.

Happiness is a by-product of following God. Happiness is not the goal.

To seek God you must do it with all your heart.

Seeking God means "keeping his statutes".

Matthew 6:25-31

Do not worry. Ironic. Can add a single hour to your life.

God knows your needs.

Seek first His kingdom and righteousness.

Then God will give you all you need.

Acts 17:26-27

God determines the times and places each person lives.

He does this so men will seek Him, reach out for Him and find Him.

He is not far from anyone.

A Christian meeting you is not by chance but of God.

John 4:23-24

God seeks men (Acts 17 taught men must seek God).

Men who want to worship Him in spirit and in truth.

Acts 17:10-12

Read and study the Bible for your own convictions.

Study daily.

Jeremiah 29:11-13

God has an individual plan for your life.

A plan to prosper you with hope and a future.

You will find God when you seek Him with all your heart.

Acts 8:26-39

The angels and the Holy Spirit are helping to get you to God

Do not be afraid to ask questions about life or the bible--be humble.

You need someone to explain the bible to you.

You will be "rejoicing" when you find God.

Matthew 7:7-8

Seek and God guarantees you will find Him

Ask God for help.

Which one you pick?

The Word of God

2 Timothy 3:16

All scripture is inspired by God.

It is to be applied to our lives.

Hebrews 4:12

The Word is relevant.

The Word cuts (hurts).

It is good to be cut by the Word of God b/c it will cut the sin out of your life.

2 Peter 1:20-21

There is no private interpretation of the bible.

The Holy Spirit inspired the men who wrote the books of the bible.

John 8:31-32

Intellectual belief is not enough - nor can we go by our feelings.

Everyone must hold onto and follow the teachings of Jesus to be a true disciple.

Sincerity does not equal truth.

Matthew 15:1-9

Do not go by traditions or creeds.

Worship by traditions - those which contradict the Word of God are worship in vain.

1 Timothy 4:16

Watch life and doctrine closely - they both are inseparable.

It is important to watch both of them.

Acts 17:10-12

Read bible daily.

Read to gain your own convictions.

James 1:22-25

Don't merely listen but apply it on your life and live it.

Don't deceive yourself.

Your life needs to reflect the bible.

John 12:48

Why study the bible?

Because it will judge you.

Discipleship, 3/4/2015

Matthew 28:18-20

What does Jesus want everyone to become?

Jesus came to make disciples. Only baptized disciples were saved.

Mark 1:14-18

Calling of the first disciples

"Come follow me"

Jesus gave them a purpose for living.

They left the nets at once.

Luke 9:23-26

If any man...

Deny yourself (Matt. 26:36-39) - Jesus denying himself

Carry the cross daily.

Gain the world... forfeit soul, lose your life for Jesus... save it.

Luke 14:25-33

If any man...

Count the cost (v 28-30).

Consider the alternatives (v 31-32).

Love Christ more than any person (v 26).

Persecution (v 27).

Everything, not just anything (v 33).

Luke 11:1-4

Learn to pray as the disciples.

Daily personal relationship with God (v 3); daily prayer.

John 13:34-35

Love one another.

Be an active part of fellowship.

Matthew 28:18-20

Command - make disciples

Who gets baptized? Those who chase to be disciples.

We all need people to help us mature in Christ.

This is the only way to save the world.

Persecution, 3/10/2015

2 Timothy 3:12

Everyone!! Whoever wants to live a godly life will be persecuted.

Mark 3:20-21, 31

Even your own family will persecute you.

Jesus prioritized his spiritual family more than physical.

Don't let anybody stop you from the truth.

John 7:12-13

People were saying that Jesus was deceiving others.

We are never going to know until we try it.

Acts 5:17-18, 38-42

You are going to get persecuted!!

If you're going against God you will fail.

Acts 28:21-22

All the people are talking about the sect of the 1st century church.

Everywhere the church was, there was controversy.

1 Peter 4:1-4, 12-16

People are going to think you are a weirdo.

Let's have the same attitude as Jesus.

If you get persecuted be happy about it.

John 5:16-20, 15:18-20

People are going to hate you like they did Jesus.

Haters gonna hate.

They persecuted Jesus first. That is why people hate you.
