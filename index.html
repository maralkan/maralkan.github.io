I'M PART OF acts2fellowship

acts2fellowship (a2f) is a UC Berkeley student ministry that meets every week to study the Bible and build lasting friendships.
College is the perfect time to explore the big questions of life like, "Does God exist?" and "What am I here for?"

At a2f, you'll find a safe place to ask those questions and seek answers alongside other college students.
Throughout the week, we also hang out, eat lots of good food, study hard together, go on fun, memorable trips, and just “do life” together.
